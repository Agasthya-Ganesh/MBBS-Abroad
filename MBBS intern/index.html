Why study MBBS Abroad?

Studying MBBS abroad offers affordable tuition, globally recognized degrees, modern infrastructure, diverse clinical exposure, high-quality education, no entrance exams in many countries, and better opportunities for international career growth.

Benifits:

MBBS programs abroad are often cheaper than private medical colleges in countries like India, the US, or the UK. Many universities offer scholarships and flexible payment options. Graduates can practice medicine in multiple countries after clearing licensing exams (USMLE, PLAB, FMGE, etc.). Universities abroad focus on hands-on clinical training with real patients.

Top countries:

Russia, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Philippines, Georgia, Kyrgyzstan and Egypt

Admission process and eligibility steps for Applying MBBS Abroad?

The MBBS abroad admission process involves selecting a recognized university, meeting eligibility (50% in PCB, NEET qualification), applying online, submitting documents (passport, mark sheets, NEET score, medical certificate), receiving an admission letter, applying for a student visa, paying fees, booking travel, and starting your medical education.
